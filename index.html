Crowned to Lead

Lessons in leadership from the women of the Bible — for the girl God is calling.

From Deborah to Esther, the women of Scripture led with courage, wisdom, and unshakable faith. Crowned to Lead draws their stories into a devotional journey written for girls who are learning that they, too, are called — and that real leadership begins with knowing whose they are.

Part reflection, part invitation, each chapter pairs a woman’s story with Scripture and gentle prompts that help a young reader find her own crown.

About the Author

Joyce Ajebon-Dagadu

Doctoral student · Healthcare professional · Writer

Joyce Ajebon-Dagadu writes stories that help children — and the grown-ups who love them — see themselves in the great accounts of faith, courage, and calling.

By day she is a healthcare administration professional and a doctoral student in Health Science (DHSc) at Purdue University’s College of Health and Human Sciences, where her work centers on health equity and the wellbeing of the people who keep our health systems running.

Whether she is retelling the story of Esther for a young reader or writing a devotional for the next generation of women leaders, her work returns to one conviction: that every person is called, and that courage grows when we know whose we are.
